Common Questions & Service Details

Addressing the important details on pricing, timing, and care.

How long does a full detail take?
A full detail typically takes 4-6 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating services may require 1-3 days depending on the condition of the vehicle.
How often should I detail my car?
We recommend a maintenance wash every 2 weeks and a full detail every 6-12 months to keep your protection warranty valid and your car looking new.
Is ceramic coating worth it?
Absolutely. It provides long-term protection against UV rays, chemicals, and minor scratches, while making your car much easier to clean and maintain.
Do you offer mobile services?
Yes, we have a fully equipped mobile unit for basic detailing services. However, paint correction and coatings must be done in our controlled studio environment.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Payment is due upon completion of the service.
